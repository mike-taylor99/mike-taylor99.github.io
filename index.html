About

Michael is an undergraduate student at the University of Connecticut working towards completing his Bachelor of Science in Engineering for Computer Science and Engineering. Michael will be concentrating in Software Development and Design, and is concurrently working to complete his minor in Mathematics.

Michael has a great interest in the application of mathematics underlying within algorithms, electric circuits, and physics. Michael's courseload has helped to solidify his mathematical skills, as he has achieved top marks in his Calculus and Differential Equations courses. He hopes to enhance his education to a dual degree in Applied Mathematics because of the strong correlation between Mathematics and Computer Science & Engineering.
